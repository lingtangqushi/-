文化遗产消失

Cultural heritage at risk

舟山渔歌、路易斯安那克里奥尔文化等珍贵文化正面临永久消失

Precious cultures such as Zhoushan fishing songs and Louisiana Creole culture are at risk of permanent loss

根据IPCC 2022报告，全球海平面自1900年以来已经上升了约20厘米，而这一趋势正在加速。海洋温度升高导致海水膨胀，冰川和冰盖融化进一步加剧了海平面上升。

According to the IPCC 2022 report, the global sea level has risen by approximately 20 centimeters since 1900, and this trend is accelerating. Rising ocean temperatures cause seawater to expand, while the melting of glaciers and ice sheets further exacerbates sea-level rise.

对于生活在海岸线上的社区来说，这意味着家园被淹没、生计被摧毁、文化遗产面临消失的风险。小岛屿国家尤其脆弱，它们贡献了全球不到0.5%的碳排放，却承受着90%以上的气候变化影响。

For communities living along coastlines, this means淹没的家园、摧毁的生计和消失的文化遗产。Small island nations are particularly vulnerable, contributing less than 0.5% of global carbon emissions yet bearing over 90% of the impacts of climate change.

为什么这很重要？

Why does this matter?

海岸侵蚀不仅威胁着人类居住环境，还破坏了重要的生态系统，如红树林、珊瑚礁和湿地，这些生态系统对于生物多样性和海岸保护至关重要。

Coastal erosion threatens not only human habitats but also vital ecosystems such as mangroves, coral reefs, and wetlands, which are crucial for biodiversity and coastal protection.

舟山渔村消失的十年

A Decade of Disappearing Fishing Villages in Zhoushan

根据《中国妇女报》的报道，舟山群岛的许多渔村在过去十年中因海平面上升和海岸侵蚀而逐渐消失。渔民们不得不放弃传统的渔业生计，另谋出路。当地政府正在实施搬迁计划，帮助居民适应新环境。

According to China Women's News, many fishing villages in the Zhoushan Archipelago have disappeared over the past decade due to sea-level rise and coastal erosion. Fishermen have been forced to abandon their traditional livelihoods and seek alternative sources of income. The local government is implementing relocation plans to help residents adapt to new environments.

海岸防护工程

Coastal Protection Engineering

建设海堤、防波堤等工程设施可以有效抵御海浪侵蚀和风暴潮的冲击。荷兰的"三角洲工程"是全球知名的海岸防护典范。

Constructing engineering facilities such as seawalls and breakwaters can effectively resist wave erosion and storm surges. The Netherlands' "Delta Works" is a globally recognized coastal protection model.

  • 海堤和防波堤可以显著降低沿海地区的洪水风险
  • Seawalls and breakwaters can significantly reduce flood risks in coastal areas
  • 生态海堤结合自然生态系统，提供更可持续的防护
  • Ecological seawalls integrate natural ecosystems for more sustainable protection
